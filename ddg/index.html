    Gettin Tullz

    THE RULES

    This game is meant to be played while watching competitive dota.
    • A drink is considered to be one solid sip from your cup
    • Waterfalling is considered drinking without pause (usually finishing your drink)
    • A penalty shot is a modest pre-measured amount of (preferably) foul-tasting liquid
    • A "group" is a number of players whom are sitting together and are on the same team
    SETUP:
    1. Up to 10 people should be split into two teams
    2. Each player starts with a full cup. This is your "life"
    3. There should also be at least 5 penalty shots poured and ready
    4. After the drafting phase completes, each team decides who will use which hero.
    5. When your cup is empty, (ie. you run out of life), you must take a penalty shot, unless a rule states otherwise
    6. All rules are player-enforced, you can cheat if you can get away with it
    7. If you are caught consciously cheating, you must take a penalty shot
    Drink when:
    • Your hero dies
    • Your team loses a tower
    • Your team loses a barracks
    • The enemy team kills roshan
    The Drinks above scale depending on game time:
    • 0-20 minutes: 1 drink
    • 20-40 minutes: 2 drinks
    • 40+ minutes: 3 drinks
    • The above number also determine the strength of your ultimate. ie: 4/5/6 drinks respectively.
    You are considered to have "inflicted" a drink when:
    • Your hero kills another hero
    • Your hero gets the last hit on a tower
    • You solo kill roshan
    • You use a skill that makes another player drink
    Pay attention to your hero's ability, it may affect the amount you, or people you kill must drink.
